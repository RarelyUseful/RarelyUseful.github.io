Hi, I'm Paweł.

Fullstack JavaScript Developer.

I have recently completed postgraduate in "JavaScript Developer" from Bialystok University of Technology. During it I learned how to use two most popular frameworks (React and Angular) and created my first applications. I also learned how to use Node.js and how ServerSide applications work. Talking with experienced programmers gave me great perspective on work and education and motivated me even more to learn as much is I could.

Obviously my passion for programming started earlier. I used various technologies, because as self-tought I wanted to learn few major languages before commiting to one. Most of my time i spent using Java. I even got my first job making app for "Radio ZET" using HarmonyOS (which was basically Java). That app was dedicated to Huaweii Smartwatch, so experience wasn't really smooth. I still liked it, and couldn't wait to get my hands on another project.

As soon as I finished my postgraduate, I applied for Summer Internship in SoftwareHut. I learned how to build websites using HTML and CSS and took part in larger project that used Angular to create in-house recruitment tool to improve HR efficiency. I got plenty real-life experience and mentoring and had an amazing opportunity to see how software is made from the ground up.
I'm really happy to keep doing this and I'm currently looking for career opportunity where I will be able to expand my knowledge by working on bigger projects, assisted by senior developers.
